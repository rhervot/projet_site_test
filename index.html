Top 10 rap albums of 2024 (not in a specific order)

American Dream - 21 Savage

Plan A - Lil Tecca

Released in late september, this was supposed to be a very awaited summer album than ended up releasing a bit too late. However this album is definitly one of tecca's most thought out project making it one of the best albums of the year.

2093 - YEAT

2093 by Yeat is an album that was supposed to redefine his way of making music and he succeeded at just that. This project created a whole other side of Yeat that most weren't expecting, but it was realised in such a way that the album actually sounds like it comes from the future and not in a redundant wat either. This opened up the door for his new project "LYFESTYLE" that actually dropped during the making of this page.
